ENDORSEMENTS

Over the past four years, I’ve built a coalition of justice communities from education to climate action to labor rights and housing to build a winning agenda from inside City Hall. Together, we’ve won historic investments in our schools and kids, new laws protecting workers, and policies that uphold housing as a human right. This is the energy moving our city forward.

It’s why the Philadelphia Inquirer endorsed me as the only incumbent at-large member of City Council, writing that I am a “savvy, passionate, and progressive leader on worker rights, education and housing.”

I’m proud to be endorsed by over 40 organizations that showcase the diversity of our City.
